Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Pulse ("the App"). Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

1. Data Controller

The App is developed and distributed by:

Qian Gao
Germany

Contact:

tara_indy_consulting@icloud.com

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed with a privacy-first approach.

The App does not collect, transmit, store, sell, rent, share, or otherwise process personal information on external servers.

All financial records, categories, notes, settings, and related data entered into the App are stored locally on your device unless you choose to use Apple's iCloud services.

3. Local Data Storage

The App stores user-generated content locally on the user's device.

Examples include:

  • Income records
  • Expense records
  • Budget information
  • Financial notes
  • User preferences
  • Application settings

This information remains under your control.

The developer does not have access to this data.

4. iCloud Backup and Synchronization

The App may optionally support backup and restoration using Apple's iCloud services.

If enabled by the user:

  • Data may be stored in the user's personal iCloud account.
  • Data transmission and storage are handled by Apple.
  • The developer cannot access data stored in the user's iCloud account.

Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's own policies.

5. Analytics and Tracking

The App does not:

  • Track users
  • Profile users
  • Monitor behavior
  • Use advertising identifiers
  • Use third-party analytics services
  • Use cookies
  • Use fingerprinting technologies

No analytics or advertising SDKs are integrated into the App.

6. Location Information

The App does not collect, access, or process location information.

7. Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app purchases through Apple's App Store.

Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple.

The developer does not receive payment card information or other financial account details.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International Users

The App may be available in multiple countries.

Because the App does not collect personal information, cross-border transfers of personal data by the developer do not occur.

10. GDPR Information (European Economic Area)

For users located within the European Economic Area (EEA):

The App generally does not process personal data.

Where the user voluntarily stores information within the App or uses iCloud backup services, such processing occurs under the user's control.

If you have questions regarding privacy, please contact the developer using the contact information above.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Changes become effective upon publication of the updated version.

The "Last Updated" date will indicate the latest revision.
