Privacy Policy

Pulse - Cash Flow Manager is designed to help you record and understand personal finances with a privacy-first approach.

Information We Collect

Pulse may store the financial records you enter in the app, such as transaction amounts, categories, notes, dates, income, budgets, and account labels. This information is used to provide the app's core bookkeeping features.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used to display summaries, charts, budgets, and transaction history inside Pulse. We do not sell your personal information.

Local Storage and iCloud

Pulse is intended to store app data on your device and, where enabled by the app or system settings, through Apple services such as iCloud. Apple handles iCloud data according to Apple's own terms and privacy policy.

Diagnostics

If Apple provides crash reports or diagnostic information, we may use that information to improve app stability and performance. These reports are handled through Apple's developer tools.

Third-Party Services

The MVP website does not use advertising trackers. If the app later integrates third-party services, this policy will be updated before or when those services are introduced.

Children's Privacy

Pulse is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact us through the support page.
