Getting started

Pulse Guide

Use Pulse as a lightweight daily ledger: record transactions when they happen, review your month, and adjust categories as your habits change.

1

Set your monthly context

Start by confirming the month you want to track. Add expected income or opening balance if the app version supports it.

2

Add expenses as they happen

Enter the amount, select a category, add an optional note, and save. Short notes make later review easier.

3

Review your cash flow

Use the overview screen to compare income, spending, and remaining balance for the current month.

4

Keep categories useful

Use categories that match real decisions, such as groceries, rent, transport, subscriptions, and savings.
