Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a transaction?

Open Pulse, tap the add button, enter the amount, choose a category, then save.

Can I edit or delete a record?

Open the transaction detail view and use the edit or delete action provided in the app.

Where is my data stored?

Pulse is designed to keep your bookkeeping data on your device and, where enabled, through Apple services such as iCloud.
